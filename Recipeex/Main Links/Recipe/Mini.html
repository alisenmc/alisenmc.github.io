Mini Sesame Zucchini Loaves



                                                                                                 
                                          


Mini Sesame Zucchini Loaves in this hot summer, why not heat up your ovens and bake tasty Zucchini loaves moist Zucchini loaves fresh out of the oven. These zucchini loaves will surely make your mornings fresh and happy.


Ingredients


  • 2 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 c. olive oil, plus more for pans
  • 2 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 c. granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1/2 c. packed brown sugar
  • 1 large lemon
  • 12 oz. zucchini, coarsely grated (about 21/2 cups)
  • 2 tbsp. white sesame seeds
  • 2 large eggs, at room temp
  • 1 c. walnuts, toasted and chopped
  • Butter, for serving
