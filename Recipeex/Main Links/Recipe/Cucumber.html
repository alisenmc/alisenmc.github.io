Cucumber Lime Smoothie



                                                                                                 
                                          


This refreshing cucumber lime smoothie recipe combines cucumber and lime juice with honey and ice to create a flavor combination that will refresh you even on the hottest summer days. It is great for hydration, detox, digestion, and weight loss.


Ingredients


  • 2 cucumbers, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 cup honey or sugar
  • Up to 2 cups water or ice, (optional)
  • Small handful mint, (optional)


Preparation


  • Place all ingredients in a smoothie-style blender and puree until smooth.
  • Taste and adjust as needed.
  • Garnish with a wedge of lime or sprig of mint (optional) and enjoy cold.
